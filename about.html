About CoolAir

Your trusted partner for all air conditioning needs since 1995

Our Mission

At CoolAir, our mission is to provide exceptional air conditioning services that enhance the comfort, health, and energy efficiency of homes and businesses throughout the Phoenix area. We are committed to:

  • Delivering superior quality workmanship on every job
  • Providing honest, transparent, and fair pricing
  • Employing highly trained, certified technicians
  • Recommending energy-efficient solutions
  • Building lasting relationships with our customers

Our Vision

We envision a future where every home and business in Phoenix enjoys optimal indoor comfort while minimizing environmental impact. Our vision includes:

  • Being the most trusted AC service provider in Arizona
  • Leading the industry in adopting eco-friendly technologies
  • Continuously improving our service standards
  • Expanding our services to help more communities

Our History

2015

CoolAir was founded by Lorah Hernandez, a certified HVAC technician with a vision to provide reliable air conditioning services to Phoenix residents.

2016

Expanded our team to 10 technicians and moved to a larger facility to accommodate our growing business.

2017

Received the "Best HVAC Service Provider" award from the Phoenix Business Association for our commitment to excellence.

2018

Launched our eco-friendly initiative, focusing on energy-efficient systems and environmentally responsible practices.

2025

Celebrating over 9 years of service with a team of 30+ certified technicians and thousands of satisfied customers across Cavite/Philippines.
